# BA Report — VCST-5009 Skyflow Cart Payment (AllowCartPayment)
**Date:** 2026-06-05 | **Scope:** End-user documentation, VCST-5009, theme 2.51.0-pr-2308 | **Env:** vcst-qa

## Executive Summary

Feature VCST-5009 (AllowCartPayment) moves the Skyflow credit-card form from a separate Payment page onto the cart page, matching the existing CyberSource single-page checkout pattern. This report delivers a publish-ready end-user guide for the new flow, covering both the saved-card and new-card paths, decline/retry behavior, and guest checkout. Three statements in the existing Virto Commerce Storefront User Guide now describe the old behavior and must be updated before the next public docs release.

---

## 1. User Guide — Paying by card on the cart page (Skyflow)

<!-- Audience: shoppers. Zero technical jargon. -->

### Introduction

When you choose **Bank card (Skyflow)** as your payment method, a secure card form appears directly on the cart page. You do not need to navigate to a separate Payment page — you fill in your card details and place your order in one step.

Your card details are sent directly to the secure payment vault. The store never sees or stores your raw card number.

![Cart page with Skyflow payment section expanded](../../../tests/Sprint26-11/VCST-5009/screenshots/A-newcard-form-empty-desktop.png)
*The Skyflow card form appears inline on the cart page once you select Bank card (Skyflow).*

---

### Prerequisites

- Your cart must contain at least one item.
- A shipping address must be set.
- If you are paying with a **different billing address**, uncheck **Same as shipping address** and select or add a billing address before the card form appears.

---

### Path 1 — Pay with a saved card

Use this path if you have previously saved a card to your account.

1. On the cart page, open the **Payment method** dropdown and select **Bank card (Skyflow)**.
2. The payment section expands below the order summary. You will see a **Saved cards** dropdown with the placeholder "Select credit card".
3. Open the **Saved cards** dropdown and select the card you want to use. Cards are shown as masked numbers, for example **•••• 0015 (09/27)**.
4. A **Security code** (CVV) field appears beneath the dropdown.

   ![Saved-card CVV field](../../../tests/Sprint26-11/VCST-5009/screenshots/B-savedcard-cvv-empty-desktop.png)
   *After selecting a saved card, enter the security code from the back of the card.*

5. Enter the **Security code** printed on the back of your card (usually 3 digits; 4 digits for American Express).
6. The **Place order** button becomes active once the security code is valid.
7. Click **Place order**.
8. On success you will see: **"Payment successful — Order CO… has been successfully submitted"**.

   ![Order completed confirmation](../../../tests/Sprint26-11/VCST-5009/screenshots/F-2-order-completed-skyflow.png)
   *The success page confirms your order number and that payment was accepted.*

9. Your order is now visible at **Account → Orders**.

!!! note "Why do I need to enter the CVV every time?"
    Your saved card stores only the masked card number and expiry date — never the security code. Re-entering the CVV each time is a security requirement that prevents unauthorized charges if your account is ever accessed by someone else.

!!! note "Switching saved cards"
    If you select a different saved card, the CVV field clears and the **Place order** button is disabled again. Enter the CVV for the newly selected card to continue.

---

### Path 2 — Pay with a new card

Use this path if you do not have a saved card, or want to pay with a card not yet in your account.

1. On the cart page, open the **Payment method** dropdown and select **Bank card (Skyflow)**.
2. The payment section expands. Open the **Saved cards** dropdown and select **Add new card**.
3. A secure card form appears with four fields:

   | Field | What to enter |
   |---|---|
   | **Card number** | Your 16-digit (or 15-digit Amex) card number — no spaces needed |
   | **Cardholder name** | Name exactly as it appears on the card |
   | **Expiration date** | Month and year in MM / YY format |
   | **Security code** | The 3- or 4-digit code on the back (or front for Amex) |

4. As you type the card number, the card-brand icon updates automatically (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, etc.).
5. Each field validates in real time. If a field is incomplete or invalid, helper text appears beneath it.
6. The **Place order** button is disabled until all four fields are valid. The label "Complete all required information to proceed" appears below the form while fields are incomplete.
7. When all fields are valid, click **Place order**.
8. On success you will see: **"Payment successful — Order CO… has been successfully submitted"**.
9. Your order is visible at **Account → Orders**.

!!! note "Your card details are secure"
    The card form runs inside a secure frame. Your card number, expiry date, and security code are sent directly to the payment vault — they never pass through the store's servers.

---

### If your payment is declined

If the bank declines your card, you are taken to a **Payment retry page** (breadcrumb: **Back to order details**). Your order has already been created — there are no duplicate orders.

![Payment decline retry page](../../../tests/Sprint26-11/VCST-5009/screenshots/F-4-decline-redirect-payment-page.png)
*The retry page lets you correct your card details and pay for the same order.*

On the retry page:

1. Review the error message: **"Payment failed — Order CO… has not been paid — There is a problem with your payment. Please try again."**
2. Correct your card details — for example, re-check the expiry date or security code.
3. Click **Pay now**.

If the retry succeeds, you will see the standard order confirmation. If it fails again, repeat the steps or try a different card.

**Coming back later:** your unpaid order is visible at **Account → Orders** with the status **Payment required**. Open the order and click **Pay now** at any time to complete payment.

---

### Paying as a guest

You do not need an account to use Skyflow. The card form is available during guest checkout, but only after a billing address is provided.

- If **Same as shipping address** is checked, the card form appears once a shipping address is set.
- If you uncheck **Same as shipping address** and have not added a billing address yet, you will see "Select a billing address" before the card form appears. Add a billing address to proceed.

!!! note
    Guests cannot save cards for future use. To save cards, [create an account](../account/create-account.md) and go to **Account → Saved credit cards**.

### Frequently asked questions

**Why is the Place order button disabled?**
The button stays disabled until all required card fields are filled in correctly, or — for a saved card — until you have entered the CVV. The helper text "Complete all required information to proceed" appears while the form is incomplete.

**I don't see the Skyflow option in the payment dropdown. What should I do?**
Check that your cart is not empty and that a shipping address is set. If Skyflow is still missing, contact the store's support team.

**Do I have to go to a separate page to enter my card?**
No. With the current checkout, the Skyflow card form appears directly on the cart page. You complete everything in one step.

**What if I use multi-step checkout?**
Some store configurations use a multi-step checkout. In that case, the Skyflow card form appears at the **Billing** step, and payment is confirmed on the **Payment** step. The card form itself works the same way.

## 2. Docs updates needed

The following statements in the existing Virto Commerce Storefront User Guide describe the **old** Skyflow behavior and must be revised before the next public docs release.

| Existing page | Current statement | Required change |
|---|---|---|
| `storefront/user-guide/shopping/checkout-process` — Bank card (Skyflow) tab | "SkyFlow adds the Payment step to the checkout process" | Replace with: "Bank card (Skyflow) displays the card form directly on the cart page — no separate Payment step is needed for single-page checkout." |
| `storefront/user-guide/shopping/checkout-process` — Step 7 (or equivalent) describing the Skyflow path | Any step that says "After clicking Place order you are redirected to the Payment page to fill in your bank card details" — as it applies to Skyflow | Revise to: "Fill in your card details in the Skyflow section on the cart page, then click Place order." Remove the redirect step for the Skyflow payment method in single-page checkout. |
| `storefront/user-guide/shopping/checkout-process` — Payment method comparison table or note (if present) | Any text listing Skyflow as a processor that requires a separate payment page redirect | Update to align Skyflow with the CyberSource single-page pattern: "The card form appears directly on the cart page." |

!!! note "Multi-step checkout"
    The updates above apply to stores using **single-page checkout** (the default). For stores with `checkout_multistep_enabled`, Skyflow still uses a separate Billing + Payment step sequence — that existing description remains accurate for that configuration. Docs should distinguish the two configurations explicitly.

## 3. Open questions for the docs and product team

1. **Default payment method:** The verified behavior shows the payment dropdown defaults vary by store configuration. Should the user guide specify which method is pre-selected by default, or instruct users to "open the dropdown and select Bank card (Skyflow)" without assuming a default?

2. **Multi-step vs single-page wording:** The user guide needs a clear signpost telling readers which behavior applies to their store. Is there a user-visible indicator (URL pattern, page title, step indicator) that reliably distinguishes single-page checkout from multi-step checkout?

3. **Saved-card deletion:** Can users delete individual saved cards, or only manage them from the Saved credit cards page? The current verified behavior shows the dropdown list but does not cover deletion flow — the cross-link to Saved credit cards should be confirmed as complete.

4. **Guest card tokenization scope:** Are guest card entries tokenized and discarded after the order, or is there any guest-to-account upgrade path that would retain the card token? Clarifying this shapes the guest-checkout note.

5. **"Pay now" button on unpaid orders:** Is the **Pay now** button on the Account → Orders detail page Skyflow-specific, or does it appear for all declined/unpaid payment methods? The guide may need a unified "Paying for an unpaid order" section rather than per-processor coverage.
