## 2. User Flow Analysis

Two operator flows are exposed by the build under test:

- **Silent flow** — operator is signed in to the storefront AND has the `platform:security:loginOnBehalf` permission → impersonate route skips the form, shows a brief "Switching to customer view…" loader, reloads in customer context.
- **Security Verification form flow** — operator is anonymous or lacks the permission → form re-authenticates the operator with title "Security Verification — Confirm your identity to continue", subtitle "You are logging in on behalf of {target email}. The session will be audited.", current-email + password fields, "Verify and continue" / "Cancel" buttons.

The user guide explains both flows in operator-friendly language; engineering detail (route guards, token grants) is omitted.
